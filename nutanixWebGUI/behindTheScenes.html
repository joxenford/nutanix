If you are curious how I am accomplishing what seems to be an interactive web console
without having the web console accessing an actual Nutanix cluster, I am simply using
images, or screen captures, and creating clickable areas (imagemaps for the hardcore
geeks) that link to other pages with images. This is also why the screen may appear
to shift around occasionally.

Because of this, and the fact that this type of thing is not my job and I am doing this
in my spare time as a way to show people the Nutanix Prism interface without the worry
of said system being botched up in any way, the functionality at any given moment may
be very limited and subject to change!

If you are viewing this on a laptop/desktop computer in a browser, your task of figuring
out what works will be MUCH easier. Simply hover the pointer over any item in the image
and if it changes to a hand, you can click on that item and it will do something.

If you are using a mobile device like a phone or tablet, then you will have to resort to
trial and error to locate items that will have an action associated with it. Hunt and peck. ;-)

Also keep in mind that, because each and every action is a new page and image, some things
may take substantially longer to load than would happen on an actual working Nutanix
cluster's Web Console. If I have more free time, or help from others, I will attempt
to make this much more user friendly and functional.
